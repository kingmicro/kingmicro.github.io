I'm an Assistant Professor (Lecturer - UK equivalent) at the University of Southampton, a research-intensive Russell Group University. My group seeks to understand: (i) the basic processes that determine root-microbe interactions in the rhizosphere and rhizoplane, (ii) how can we leverage microbes to improve plant fitness under drought stress, and (iii) the biotic interactions that govern microbial community assembly.
