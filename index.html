Welcome to the King Lab Website!
Dr. William L King

We're the KingMicroLab at the University of Southampton. Our research explores root–microbe interactions, microbial community assembly, and how soil microbiomes can be leveraged to improve plant fitness under environmental stress. We combine bench microbiology with large-data omics to ask "how do organisms interact with each other and does this change under stress?"

Broad Research Interests

Root–microbe interactions

Microbial community assembly

Soil microbiomes & plant fitness

Stress responses & resilience
